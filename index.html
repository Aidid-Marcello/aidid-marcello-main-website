Founder & Host
20+ years in front of — and behind — the camera

From the TV3 gallery
to a studio that's my own.

I came up the old way — executive producer at TV3, learning television in the gallery where every second counts and nothing airs twice. Two decades of live shows, formats and productions taught me how a story holds an audience.

So I built the room I always wished I'd had: Mindquest Digital Solution — MQ Digital, a studio where I direct, produce and host on my own terms. Same instinct for story. New tools, new platforms, no permission needed.
